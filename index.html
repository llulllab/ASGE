The signal sentiment classifiers discard.

ASGE is a computational engine for analysing ambivalence structures in natural language. The sustained co-presence of opposing orientations toward the same object, before it stabilises into observable behaviour.

Standard sentiment classifiers flatten that ambiguity into positive, negative, or neutral. The signal that actually precedes escalation sits in the gap those models discard. ASGE adds a layer existing OSINT and risk toolchains do not provide.

Architecture

A three-stage computational pipeline.

Three stages. Speed, time, depth. The internal mechanics are not the product. The outputs are.

01 — In-stream

Real-time signal capture during live discourse.

02 — Temporal

Minute-by-minute density mapping across wave, bar, and delta projections.

03 — Deep

Structured analytical synthesis. Peak concentration moments and dominant themes.

Use

Built for analysts who read what others discard.

Sustained ambivalence structures act as early indicators of neurotic crisis at the individual level, offensive escalation at the collective level, and criminal action across both, before they stabilise into observable behaviour. ASGE adds a layer existing OSINT and risk toolchains do not provide.

Posture

How we work, briefly.

ASGE is built for analyst augmentation and early-warning. Customers keep humans in the loop and operate within EU and partner-state frameworks. We work openly with academic, policy, and commercial research customers, and selectively with security and defence-adjacent buyers.

We do not support surveillance, profiling, or targeting of named individuals. We do not integrate into autonomous lethal or kinetic decision systems. We do not deploy in or export to jurisdictions under active EU human-rights sanctions. Use cases that conflict with that posture are declined.

EU data residency. GDPR-aligned. Customer transcripts are not retained by ASGE; only session metadata required for usage accounting.
